Madero Tango is an **iconic Buenos Aires show** that combines tango, live music, dance and gastronomy in a large-scale production. Located in Puerto Madero, it offers a modern and sophisticated staging that traces the history and passion of Argentine tango, from its origins to more contemporary expressions.

With top-level dancers, a live orchestra and a carefully crafted visual proposal, Madero Tango stands as a complete cultural and tourist experience — ideal for those seeking a first approach to tango in a spectacular setting.
