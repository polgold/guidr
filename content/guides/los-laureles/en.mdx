Los Laureles is the quintessential historic *tanguería* of Barracas and one of Buenos Aires' **Bares Notables** (heritage-listed bars). Founded in **1893**, it preserves the essence of the old Buenos Aires *arrabal*: original floors, walls full of memory, and live tango.

Declared a Site of Cultural Interest and Historical Heritage, it is a place where milonga, live music, and neighborhood tradition still beat with authenticity.
