La Flor de Barracas is a historic *bodegón* in the south of Buenos Aires, deeply tied to the identity of the neighborhood and the memory of tango. Traditional and unpretentious, it preserves the spirit of working-class Buenos Aires: shared tables, simple cooking, and the warm atmosphere of a true neighborhood meeting place.

More than a restaurant, it is a space where local history remains alive. The original floors from the late 1800s blend seamlessly with the bar, and every corner holds the soul of old Buenos Aires.
